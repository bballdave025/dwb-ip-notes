# IP Classification Framework

This document classifies projects in this repository to clarify intent,
reduce ambiguity, and support responsible intellectual-property handling
across employment contexts.

Projects are grouped into three categories.

---

## A. Independent Research

Projects developed independently and intended for academic publication
or open research.

Characteristics:

- Core ideas developed prior to or independently of employment
- Work conducted on personal time
- No use of employer systems, datasets, or confidential information
- Based on public data, personal data, or independently created materials
- Intended for publication, collaboration, or open dissemination

Purpose:

To preserve the ability to conduct and publish independent research
without ambiguity regarding ownership or scope.

---

## B. Personal ML Learning Experiments

Projects created as part of personal study, skill-building, or exploration
of machine-learning concepts.

Characteristics:

- Motivated by general learning, curiosity, or technical development
- May be inspired by publicly known concepts or general training experiences
- Not derived from proprietary systems or internal implementations
- Typically smaller in scope and not necessarily intended for publication

Purpose:

To document technical growth and experimentation without asserting
research ownership or external publication intent.

---

## C. Shareable Experiments

Projects that may be of practical value in applied settings and could
potentially be shared with an employer or collaborators.

Characteristics:

- Exploratory tools, workflows, or ideas
- Not tied to proprietary data or systems
- Not intended as exclusive personal research
- Can be voluntarily shared without ownership concerns

Purpose:

To enable collaboration and practical use without creating IP conflict
or ambiguity.

---

## Notes

- Classification is based on intent, origin of ideas, and resource usage.
- A project may evolve between categories over time.
- This framework exists to support clarity, not to assert exclusivity.

This document is for internal organization and IP hygiene.
